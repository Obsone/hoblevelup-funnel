Frequently Asked Questions

Before Getting Started

Some common questions to help you move forward with confidence.

Do I need a GoHighLevel account?

expand_more

Not right away. The first step is understanding where your business is now, how leads are coming in, and what kind of follow-up system actually makes sense. If GoHighLevel is the right fit, I can guide you through the setup. If you already have tools in place, we will look at the cleanest path forward first.

What types of businesses do you work with?

expand_more

I specialize in helping high-ticket service providers, coaches, and consultants who manage a high volume of leads and appointments.

Can you work with my existing GoHighLevel setup?

expand_more

Yes. I can review your current GoHighLevel setup, identify what is working, clean up what is causing friction, and rebuild only the parts that need improvement. That may include pipelines, calendars, forms, workflows, automations, tags, or follow-up sequences, depending on what your business actually needs.

How long does a typical system build take?

expand_more

Most builds are completed within 2-4 weeks, depending on the complexity of your current workflows and required integrations.

What happens during the discovery call?

expand_more

It's a low-pressure conversation to audit your current lead process, identify where you're losing revenue, and see if a custom system is the right fit.

Do you provide support after the launch?

expand_more

Absolutely. I provide documentation and a free 10-day hand-off training period for your team. For ongoing optimization, I also offer monthly retainer packages with continuous improvements and priority support.

What if I need help that you do not offer?

expand_more

I still encourage you to book a call. From there, I can understand what kind of support you need, point you toward the right specialist or tool if needed, and make sure any outside work fits into your system instead of adding more confusion.
